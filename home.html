Our Smarty Program

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program provides a caring and joyful environment where children learn through play, creativity, and exploration. We focus on building confidence, basic skills, and a love for learning from an early age.

Primary School

Our Primary program builds a strong foundation in reading, writing, math, and life skills. We guide students to think critically, behave ethically, and grow into active learners in a supportive classroom.

Middle School

Our Middle School program helps students grow academically and personally. We encourage independent thinking, teamwork, and responsibility, preparing them for high school and beyond.

High School

Our High School program prepares students for university and future careers. We focus on academic excellence, leadership, and strong values, helping students succeed in a global society.

A Brighter Future
For Your Children

SRSL Scool was officially authorized by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports on March 7, 2019. The school is managed by a Board of Trustees, which ensures quality, long-term planning, and effective administration.

Our mission is to provide high-quality education that combines academic excellence with strong values. We aim to nurture students with ethics, creativity, integrity, and international awareness, preparing them to succeed in school and in life.

SRSL Scool offers a full education from Kindergarten to Grade 12, following a curriculum aligned with national standards. Our team of experienced teachers is committed to student success through care, dedication, and professional teaching. With spacious classrooms, modern equipment, and a safe, supportive environment, we create a place where every child can learn, grow, and shine.
